Course Details

There is always the potential for emergencies to occur at your or any other facility. In order to reduce exposure to emergencies, and to lessen their impacts on your business, you must have a written Emergency Response Action Plan. Emergency action plans are developed to provide guidelines on what actions to take if an emergency should occur.
