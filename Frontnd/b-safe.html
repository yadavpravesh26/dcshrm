The B-SAFE app

The B-SAFE app is a total safety solution that works for all types of businesses. Whether you’re a start-up that has no safety and need a place to start or you have a highly developed safety program that you’d like to digitize and utilize in a paperless “app” environment. Our “B-SAFE” app provides all of the tools that professionals are looking for to train their employees on a platform that is flexible and searchable.

If you are looking for a cost effective efficient way to train employees, look no further. B-SAFE offers training modules with the required quizzes to test your employees as well as keeping you OSHA compliant.

B-SAFE was developed by safety professionals and business owners/managers to create a unique approach to safety that values time and results.

Why B-Safe

B-Safe allows you to pick the topics that pertain to your businesses needs.

B-Safe lets employees progress at their pace by allowing access to individual modules from anywhere there is an internet connection.

B-SAFE allows training in a group setting with a trainer/supervisor presenting the material and employees being able to follow along on the app as well as take a quiz in real time or at their own pace or as the material is being presented.

B-SAFE maintains a searchable database for all of your employees and the training modules they’ve completed for as long as you’re a member.

B-SAFE can document and save trainings and signatures for group trainings.

“I chose B-SAFE because it offered a solution that eliminates paper. I present the topic and my employees can follow along with the app answering the questions as we go. At the end of the training everything is already archived.”

I was paying a safety consultant $90/hr to come and keep me compliant. Now B-SAFE does this for a low monthly subscription fee. It doesn’t matter how busy we are, the trainings are ready when we are…

B-SAFE made sense because all of the topics are there when I want them. If I want to train on fall protection three times in a row, there is nothing stopping me.

New hire safety training was always so difficult because it would take the new hire’s time and that of a supervisor or manager to get it done. Now a manager does a site orientation and the employee is directed by the app regarding the modules that need to be done. I choose what they train on and when without sacrificing the production of a highly paid manager.

We had a great safety program that we’d spent years developing and we didn’t want to throw that out to get an online training solution. B-SAFE allowed us to upload our program and have our employees train on the same things they’d been accustomed to in a new format. Being able to push a training and subsequent documentation out onto my employees phones was the coolest thing ever.

B-SAFE is the product of 20 years of safety management. There are many products out there that provide safety solutions, but B-SAFE is the only platform that was developed to create a culture of safety. The days of “safety first” can’t continue because whatever comes second throws safety out the window. We encourage our clients to adopt the motto: “safety in everything we do.” If an employee finds value in their training is rewarded for following it, the right way will become the only way they do the job.

Unlimited access to our tool box topics.

Access to safety program templates.

Access to all of your employees completed quizzes, tests and documentation.
